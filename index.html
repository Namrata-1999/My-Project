Gallery

This is a Gallery element. It can behave as a lightbox (when given the lightbox class), and you can customize its style and size, as well as
assign it an optional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details).

Items

This is an Items element, and it's basically just a grid for organizing items of various types. You can customize its
style and size, as well as assign it an optional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details).
